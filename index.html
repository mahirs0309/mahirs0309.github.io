Our Organization

[One liner about why fhsmun is great]

What is Model UN?

Model UN is a prestigious program in which students have the opportunity to fill the shoes of diplomats and politicians in our modern day world, as well known political figures throughout history during conferences or trips conducted by the schools Model UN program and the conference itself. Model UN expands students knowledge on topics and issues prevalent in todays world, and leaves students with an everlasting experience. 

What is So Great About the Franklin High School Model UN Program:?

Model UN has remained a prosperous club within Franklin for many years, and has seen great success on a national level. Students have entered the club unable to speak in front of small audiences. And those same students have left being able to tell the story that they themselves have spoken in the United Nations Building in New York City in front of hundreds of delegates. Language is an art form, and here is where you master it.

What Does Model UN Mean to Students?

There is nowhere else you can gain the companionship that you find in Model UN. Students also experience a vast increase in maturity and the development of character, as can be said by any past students that has ever been involved with our program. Students also my find the inspiration to do anything great, from changing laws in reality, to changing the very world we live in. Model UN instills confidence in students that will carry them throughout their entire lives, and their experiences only serve them further.
